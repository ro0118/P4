Our start.

This all began as a dream of two young eighth graders growing up with a love for a good sub. The dream became reality with the first sub shop opening in Bayonne, NJ in 2019. Ever since, the Crunchy Sub craze has taken the area by storm. Come visit us sometimes and see what the hype is about!
